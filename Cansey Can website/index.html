About Us

About Casey

Casey Can is a Home Services company based in Madison, Mississippi. If you are located in the Madison, Jackson, or Brandon area give us a call about any of your home or yard needs. We specialize in irrigation such as drains and sprinkler systems, but we can also lay sod, handle flower beds, remove trees/shrubs, brick and mortar repair, fence repairs, build stone structures, and much more. Call the Office at 601-896-7800 to request our services and get a quote!
